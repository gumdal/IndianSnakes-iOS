This app and the website is developed and maintained by Ramakrishna Mission, Shivanahalli.

1984 All Karnataka Youth Convension had occurred at the Ramakrishna Math, Bangalore (then Sri Ramakrishna Ashrama, Bangalore). Around 3000 delegates participated and a decision to undertake Rural Development work in a nearby village was taken. In August 1984 a mobile medical unit was started. In January 1985 "Sri Ramakrishna Grama Kshema Trust" by the devotees of the Math was formed. In May 1985 Sri Ramakrishna Vidya Kendra, a Kannada primary school upto Std IV (now upto std. VII) was started. In November 1996 Ramakrishna Grama Kshema Trust was dissolved and all the activities with the assets were handed over to Ramakrishna Mission, Shivanahalli

Ramakrishna Mission still actively involves in many Rural Development and social activities like Sri Sharada Devi Chikitsa Kendra (Free Hospital), Sri Ramakrishna Vidya Kendra (Free School), Self Help Groups, Vocationl Training, Afforestation programmes such as Project Kaanana, distribution of grains to oldage people etc.
