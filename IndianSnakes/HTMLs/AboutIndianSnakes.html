This app is powered with data from www.indiansnakes.org.

Meeting place for all those who are interested in Snakes of India.
Members can share their Photos, Videos, Rescue experiences, Snake bite cases and all related matters to understand them better for their conservation and positive relations with humans.

We support balance between ethical, sensitive, scientific way of treatment so our way is flexible and so members need to keep visiting for better idea of snake world.

We don't encourage these things:
- New group of rescue and training of rescue/capturing snakes.
- Snake breeding or its knowledge.
- Snake handling or misbehaving photos.
- Big part of photos from Internet specially those without any particular reason to post for discussion.
- Snake bite treatment by commenting on symptoms.

Following are the things Admins and other members expect:
- Location and other details of content.
- Humble, curious, supportive and generous response.
- More scientific/educative and less "typical snake lover" sign from each other.

If you feel you could contribute to the scientific data and be a part of the community, then please write to: jose@indiansnakes.org. If you want to provide feedbacks, critics and suggestions about the app, please write to: support@indiansnakes.org.
